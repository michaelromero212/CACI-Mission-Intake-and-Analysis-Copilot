SECTION 1: COLLECTION REQUIREMENT OVERVIEW

Priority: HIGH
Collection Discipline: SIGINT/OSINT Fusion
Geographic Focus: Eastern European Theater
Time Sensitivity: 72-hour reporting cycle

The customer requires enhanced intelligence collection and analysis support focusing on adversary military logistics and supply chain activities. This requirement supports ongoing theater-level situational awareness and feeds into the Joint Intelligence Preparation of the Operational Environment (JIPOE).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 2: SPECIFIC INFORMATION REQUIREMENTS (SIRs)

SIR-001: Identify and track rail transport movements within the designated area of interest (AOI). Priority focus on military cargo classifications and temporal patterns.

SIR-002: Monitor open-source indicators of fuel depot activity, including commercial satellite imagery analysis and social media intelligence (SOCMINT).

SIR-003: Correlate SIGINT reporting with HUMINT collection to validate supply line interdiction opportunities.

SIR-004: Develop pattern-of-life analysis for key logistics nodes using multi-INT fusion.
