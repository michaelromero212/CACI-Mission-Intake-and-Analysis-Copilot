INTELLIGENCE COLLECTION REQUIREMENTS (ICR) UPDATE
Priority Intelligence Requirements for Q1 2025
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Classification: UNCLASSIFIED

STANDING INFORMATION NEEDS (SINs)

The following information needs remain in effect for all collection assets and analytical elements:

SIN-001: Foreign Military Capabilities
Priority: HIGH
Description: Collect information on adversary military modernization programs, including but not limited to hypersonic weapons development, advanced submarine capabilities, and anti-satellite systems.
Collection Discipline: SIGINT, GEOINT, OSINT
Reporting Cadence: Weekly

SIN-002: Counterterrorism - Homegrown Violent Extremism
Priority: CRITICAL
Description: Monitor and report on domestic radicalization pathways, attack planning indicators, and foreign terrorist organization recruitment of U.S. persons.
Collection Discipline: HUMINT, SIGINT, OSINT
Reporting Cadence: Daily

SIN-003: Cyber Threat Actor TTPs
Priority: HIGH
Description: Document tactics, techniques, and procedures of nation-state cyber actors targeting U.S. critical infrastructure sectors.
Collection Discipline: SIGINT, Cyber Intelligence
Reporting Cadence: As collected

NAMED AREA OF INTEREST (NAI) UPDATES

NAI-ALPHA: Baltic Sea Region
New requirement to monitor naval activity and troop movements in the Baltic littorals. Increased Russian Federation military exercises have been observed.

NAI-BRAVO: South China Sea
Continue surveillance of island militarization and freedom of navigation operations. Report any new construction or weapons deployments.

NAI-CHARLIE: Sahel Region
Expand collection on transnational criminal organizations and their intersection with violent extremist organizations operating in Mali, Niger, and Burkina Faso.
